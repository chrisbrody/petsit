About Brody & Cori

Your pet's happiness is our top priority.

Hi! We’re Brody (developer, 37) and Cori (librarian, 42), lifelong animal lovers with a passion for pet care. After saying goodbye to our beloved 14-year-old rescue dog, Naia, in 2019, we started pet-sitting to combine travel with quality dog time. In 2022, we adopted two rescue dogs, Neko and Lola, who stay with Cori’s parents when we’re away, allowing us to continue pet-sitting occasionally.

With years of experience caring for our pets, including seniors with special needs, we understand the importance of trust, reliability, and compassionate care. We both work remotely, making it easy to spend plenty of time with your pets. We’ve loved exploring new neighborhoods and bonding with so many furry friends across the US, Canada, and Europe. We value inclusivity, diversity, and open-mindedness, and we warmly welcome people and pets from all backgrounds into our lives.

We organize our pet-sits through email or the TrustedHousesitters platform—feel free to contact us either way! We’re proud to have all five-star reviews on TrustedHousesitters and treat every home with the utmost care and respect. Pet-sitting is more than a service for us—it’s a way to connect with communities, enjoy the company of animals, and travel sustainably.

Let’s chat!

We’d be happy to arrange a video call to discuss your needs. Contact us through Email or TrustedHousesitters and check out our Testimonials and FAQ pages to learn more. We can’t wait to meet you and your pets!
